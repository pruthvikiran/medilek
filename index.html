Welcome to Medilek Instruments

Medilek Instruments is a sole proprietorship based business concern, which was incorporated in the year 1996 at Bengaluru, Karnataka. We are one of the reputed manufacturer, supplier and service provider of Radiant Warmer, Double Surface Photo-therapy Unit, Transport Baby Incubator and many more. Our products are the first choice of our clients due to their performance centric quality standards.

We also customize these products as per the various requirements and specifications of clients. All our products are cost effective and at the same time the quality exhibited by them is top-class. The business activities of Medilek Instruments are led by Mr. Basavaraj (Proprietor), who is a highly experienced professional. Under his able leadership and guidance, we are able to grow as a company and deliver our clients with high end products.
